Garlic Parmesan Shrimp

Delicious crispy shrimp coated in a bread crumb-Parmesan mixture. My family can't get enough of it!

I would suggest doubling the recipe if you have very large eaters because this is addictive! Serve with pasta and salad.

Ingredients

  • nonstick cooking spray
  • 1 cup dry bread crumbs
  • 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 1 poung large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter (to taste)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees, spray 9x13in casserole dish
  2. Combine bread crumbs, cheese, parsley, and garlic powder in resealable bag, add shrimp and shake.
  3. Lay shimp flat in prepared dish, sprinkle with remaining mix, drizzle butter on top.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes, switch to broil for 2-3 minutes.
